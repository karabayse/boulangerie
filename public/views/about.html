About

Named after the bustling town center of Place Boulangerie in the heart of Paris, France, Boulangerie is a Parisian bakery and café located in the center of Montclair. Pastry chef and owner, Françoise Boulanger, was educated at Le Cordon Bleu and is delighted to share a taste of Paris with all who enter the doors of Boulangerie. Allons jam!
